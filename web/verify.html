Every fact this protocol serves is content-addressed (BLAKE3 over canonical CBOR → 32-char base32 CID). Every recall is wrapped in a receipt signed by the responder's ed25519 key over a deterministic preimage. This page verifies that signature in your browser, with no call back to the issuer. Drop a fact CID, paste a receipt JSON, watch the math run.
