Every band on emem ticks at a cadence (ultra-fast hourly, fast daily, medium monthly, slow yearly, static once). At any cell, all its vintages stack into a trajectory — the time-series of a single measurement at a single place. Each row is its own signed fact with its own fact_cid; cite a single moment or the whole line. One call: POST /v1/trajectory.
