One endpoint, two views. POST /v1/state with view=encoder returns one named foundation embedding at its native dimension. view=cube returns the responder's full 1792-D voxel assembled across every wired band in canonical offset order, with per-band coverage so an agent can tell attested-zero from not-yet-materialised, and extras that preserve full Clay/Prithvi 1024-D fidelity when the cube slot truncates them. The cube is a fixed-width fingerprint; the extras carry the full payload. Pick a place, hit run, watch a real responder fill the slots in front of you.

step 3

state · view=cube

POST /v1/state

Full 1792-D voxel across every wired band, in canonical offset order. With materialize=true cold bands trigger upstream fetches. The response carries coverage[] (per-band status), state_cid (BLAKE3 over canonical LE f32 bytes), and extras[] for any encoder whose native dim exceeds its cube slot.
